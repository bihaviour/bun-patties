---
title: Secrets
description: Dev-time secret management through the OS keychain.
---

`patties secret` is a dev-only utility for storing and retrieving secrets in the
local OS keychain.

## Subcommands

- `patties secret set <key> [value]`
- `patties secret get <key> [--force]`
- `patties secret list`
- `patties secret rm <key>`
- `patties secret doctor`

## Notes

- `get` refuses to print to non-TTY stdout unless `--force` is passed
- `doctor` reports whether configured secret keys resolve from the keychain,
  `Bun.env`, or are missing
- the command is blocked outside development environments except for `doctor`

## Linux dependency

When the libsecret backend is unavailable, Patties surfaces a setup hint for
Debian and Ubuntu systems.
